What Is Queue?

House of Ajax, a group of people who truly value time. We know that we can't make up for lost hours, so we decided to work on an app that allows others to hold your place in line, like the DMV or the queue at your local coffee spot. Skip the lines, avoid the drama, and save time in your life. How much is your time worth?
